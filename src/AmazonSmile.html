Amazon "Smile" Fundraiser


The Williamsburg Music Association has enrolled in the Amazon Smile program. This allows a small portion (0.5% of your purchase price) of your purchases to be donated to WMA at no cost to you! Just visit Amazon Smile, log-in with your Amazon username and password, and select Williamsburg Music Association of Williamsburg Iowa for your charity. Same products, same price, but 0.5% of the purchase price will go to WMA. Amazon Prime members can also shop here and continue to receive the benefits of membership.

Watch a video (short, 97 seconds) to learn more about the program.

We recognize that our local merchants are SO generous in their support of Williamsburg music and should be your first choice in shopping. For items that can't be found locally, please consider this program and using your Amazon purchases to benefit WMA.
